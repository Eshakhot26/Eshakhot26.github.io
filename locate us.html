ABOUT MALL

Eastend is a multi-use commercial development project by Chitrali Properties Pvt. Ltd. that features a shopping mall (retail and entertainment areas). Eastend is part of a larger development on a 67,500 sq.m. (17 acres) plot – one of the largest properties in Aundh, Pune. Aundh is a well-developed and popular area of the city with easy access to IT – BT park at Hinjewadi and the Mumbai-Pune expressway.
